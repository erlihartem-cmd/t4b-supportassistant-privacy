Privacy Policy

T4B.SupportAssistant does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, browsing history, or user activity data.

The extension operates entirely on the user’s local environment and communicates only with:

  • http://127.0.0.1/* — local SupportAssistant backend
  • https://tools4brokers.passwork.io/* — secure Passwork integration

All clipboard, task polling, and automation actions are executed locally and initiated by the user. No data is shared with third parties or remote servers.

If you have any questions or concerns about privacy, please contact the system administrator or the Tools for Brokers Support Team.
